# Trazendo o Reino — Project System

## Project Vision

“Trazendo o Reino” is not a common online course platform.

It is a spiritual and editorial formation platform centered on:
- Christ
- The Kingdom of God
- Scripture
- transformation
- reverence

The experience must feel:
- contemplative
- cinematic
- premium
- editorial
- deep
- quiet
- intentional

NEVER:
- generic SaaS
- corporate dashboard
- typical evangelical course
- cliché religious visuals
- noisy interfaces
- exaggerated spiritual aesthetics

The platform should feel like:
- a living editorial work
- a modern contemplative experience
- a sacred digital library
- a spiritual journey built with reverence

The perception should be:
> “This was built with reverence.”

---

# Spiritual and Theological Foundation

Every product decision, interface, animation, copywriting, PDF, journey, and feature must serve the mission of the project:

- reveal Christ;
- honor the Scriptures;
- proclaim the Kingdom;
- call people to repentance;
- strengthen grace;
- form disciples;
- prepare hearts for the return of Christ.

Christ is the interpretive center of everything.

The normative foundation is:
- Tanakh (Law, Prophets, Writings)
- New Testament

The Gospels occupy the center of interpretation because they reveal the Messiah directly.

Ancient Jewish and early Christian literature may be used as:
- context
- historical background
- spiritual edification

But NEVER as:
- final authority
- replacement for Scripture
- a new gospel
- secret revelation

---

# Core Discernment Rules

Every generated experience, lesson, article, PDF, or interface must pass these filters:

- Does Christ remain at the center?
- Does grace remain firm?
- Does this confirm the Gospel or distort it?
- Does this produce repentance or merely curiosity?
- Does this align with the words of Jesus?
- Can this be confirmed by Tanakh + Christ + Apostolic teaching?
- Does this edify the heart or feed speculation?

NEVER encourage:
- salvation through secret knowledge
- obsession with codes, calendars, angels, conspiracies, or speculation
- elitist spirituality
- fear-driven spirituality
- legalism
- mystical superiority
- another gospel

The Kingdom begins with:
- repentance
- faith
- obedience
- grace
- transformation
- new birth
- discipleship

---
